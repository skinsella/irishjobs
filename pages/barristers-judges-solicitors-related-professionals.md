## Overall outlook for this sector

Employment levels in this occupational group saw some fluctuations over the five-year
period, with employment since 2023 declining by 5,000 persons, returning to 2019 levels.
Almost half of persons employed in these occupations worked in the public
administration and defence sector, with males representing three quarters of the
workforce. The outlook for employment in these occupations may vary depending on
factors such as government funding and levels of public consumption.
